Lingfield Horticultural Show

Monday 13th July 2024

Everyone is encouraged to enter which is free to all. There are several floral art classes. Arrangement of Flowers, fruit and vegetables in a basket – 600 x 600 mm Arrangement of predominantly white flowers to include a bottle of wine 450 x 450mm Arrangement of summer flowers and foliage 450 x 450 mm Miniature arrangement up to 10mm overall. If anyone is interested I can send you an entry form. Please note all cut stems to be in water or water retaining material – no floral foam (Oasis)

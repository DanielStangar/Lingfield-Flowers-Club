Monthly meeting and
Inhouse Foam Free Autumnal Workshop


Meeting and workshop will be held on Monday 30th October 2023 at The Community Centre Lingfield door opened from 7.00pm.

The workshop will be run by local floral designer Emma Fuller-Wilke. 

You will need to bring a watertight vase or other container with an opening of 15-20 cm.  Please also being some foliage such as eucalyptus, hebe, pittosporum or you could buy a  bunch of euc or Rucus. I am sure some people will have extra foliage if you can’t bring any. Some architectural looking twigs, twisted willow/hazel or dogwood. Autumnal Coloured flowers 10/15 stems which can be bought from your local supermarket. A metal pin holder / frog. We still have some on our sales table if you don’t have one or they are on sale from Amazon. Can you please let one of the committee know if you are attending the meeting.
